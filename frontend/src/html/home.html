About Us

The Calcutta Technical School is one of the most prestigious and oldest technical institutions in West Bengal. Established in 1927, before India's independence, it began with need-based technical courses under the Board of Apprentice Training.

Over the years, it transitioned into a Government-sponsored institution offering Diploma courses in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering. Today, it is a heritage institution taken over by the Government in 1994 — a symbol of educational legacy and technical excellence.
